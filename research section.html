Research Background

I specialize in animal cell culture and cryopreservation, with a focus on reducing transportation costs. Notable achievements include developing improved cryopreservation methods using biomatrix derivatives. Published work includes studies on flavonoids and ongoing endeavors involve mastering bioinformatics for enhanced data analysis skills. Future goals include in-depth studies of individual cells and their environment.
