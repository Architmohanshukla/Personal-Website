About Me

Hello There, I'm Archit

I am a Biomedical Engineer driven by a passion to engineer technology that directly improves human healthcare. My academic and research path bridges the gap between biotechnology, engineering physics, and clinical translation.

I completed my Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Biomedical Engineering at IIT Ropar. This intensive training empowered me to combine wet-lab biological protocols with computational tools and engineering methodologies.

Subsequently, I spent a pivotal year at IIT Delhi working with mPRAGATI (MedTech Product Development Acceleration Gateway of India). Here, I worked on the frontlines of product translation—assisting in scaling medical diagnostic concepts toward clinical reality, navigating fabrication standards, and aligning projects with ISO 13485 requirements.
